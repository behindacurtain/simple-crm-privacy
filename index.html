Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 December 2025

Simple CRM (“the app”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • User ID (provided by Firebase Authentication)

This information is used solely to authenticate you and secure your data.

App Data

The app allows you to store information such as clients, appointments, schedules, quotes, invoices, expenses, and notes. All data is created and managed by you and stored securely using Google Firebase services.

Analytics

Simple CRM uses Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve performance. This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymised form and does not personally identify you.

Notifications

The app may send optional notifications for scheduled reminders. Notification permissions are only requested after you explicitly enable reminders. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Photos

If you choose to attach receipts or images, the app will request access to photos only at the time you select an image. The app does not access your photo library without your permission.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Sync and secure your information
  • Improve app stability and performance

Your data is not sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Analytics

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are in place to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Your Rights

  • Access your data within the app
  • Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns
